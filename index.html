FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT DO I GET AND WHEN?

We currently offer three subscription plans and all boxes always ship towards the end of each month:
The Young Adult Monthly Box includes an exclusive edition of a brand new Young Adult fantasy novel and around 4 exclusive items related to the theme of the monthly box. The Young Adult Book-Only box includes an exclusive edition of a brand new Young Adult fantasy novel. The book is exactly the same as in our Young Adult Monthly Box, just without the items. The Adult Book-Only box includes an exclusive edition of a brand new Adult fantasy novel.

WHY AREN'T ANY SUBSCRIPTIONS AVAILABLE RIGHT NOW?

Whenever all available spots for one of our subscription plans are taken, we have a waitlist in place for that subscription plan. If you sign up to the waitlist, we will send you an email invitation to the respective subscription as soon as a spot becomes available to give you the chance to sign up. We send out invitations on a first come, first serve principle. If you want to sign up to one of our waitlists, please follow one or more of the links below:

  • Young Adult Subscription Box: join this waitlist ❯
  • Young Adult Book-Only: join this waitlist ❯
  • Adult Book-Only: join this waitlist ❯

HOW ARE THE BOOKS CHOSEN?

Our team of passionate readers and booklovers spends many hours reading and discussing early manuscripts every month to pick the best book titles for each box. We also work very closely with the leading publishing houses and authors to make sure we can create the most beautiful custom editions of the selected books. We don’t only promise to pick the most exciting reads every month but also make sure that the books look amazing on your bookshelf! 

WHAT OTHER PERKS DO SUBSCRIBERS GET?

Subscribers get Early Access to all Exclusive Editions that we regularly sell in the Fairy Trove online shop. Stock for these Exclusive Editions is always limited, and we give all of our active subscription holders the chance to purchase these products before anyone else.

CAN I CANCEL ANYTIME?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime in your account on our website. Simply navigate to the “Subscriptions” section in your account and click the “Cancel” button next to the active subscription that you would like to cancel. Once you have cancelled your subscription there won’t be any more automatic payments being made from your bank card.
